### Noise Parameters
- Scale: 0.02 (large blobby features for liquid look)
- Speed: 0.15 (slow evolution — liquid gold, not boiling)
- FBM: 2 octaves, persistence 0.5, lacunarity 2.0
- Render size: 150×150 (half of 300×300, upscaled by CALayer)
- Frame rate: 5 FPS (0.2s timer interval)
